Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 28, 2025

Coloring (hereinafter referred to as "we" or "the app") is committed to protecting your personal information and complying with all relevant privacy laws. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Information We Collect

This app is designed solely for coloring activities and image display. We do not collect the following types of personal information:

  • Personally identifiable information such as name, address, phone number, or email
  • Location information (e.g., GPS)
  • Device access permissions (e.g., photos, camera, microphone)
  • Sensitive user data such as advertising IDs or device identifiers

However, anonymous usage statistics and crash reports may be collected via the Google Play Console to analyze and improve app performance.

2. Third-Party Information Sharing

We do not share any user information with third parties. We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements, which is compliant with Google Play’s policies for apps directed at children.

3. Advertising Information

This app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use advertising identifiers (e.g., AAID/IDFA) to serve personalized ads.

Data that may be collected:

  • Advertising identifiers (AAID / IDFA)
  • App usage data for ad targeting
  • Ad interaction data for performance analysis

Opt out of personalized ads:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Reset advertising ID
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > Allow Apps to Request to Track (toggle off)

4. Protection of Children’s Personal Information

This app is primarily intended for children under the age of 5, including those under 13. We do not collect personal information from children, and no data is transmitted to external services, ensuring privacy protection.

5. External Links and Third-Party Services

The app does not include any external links or third-party services. All content operates offline, within the app environment only.

6. Data Security

As the app does not store or transmit any user data, there is no need for external storage or encryption. All operations are handled locally on the device, ensuring data privacy.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in response to app updates or legal changes. Any revisions will be announced through the app store listing or in-app notifications.

8. COPPA Compliance

This app complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and does not collect personal data from children under 13.
